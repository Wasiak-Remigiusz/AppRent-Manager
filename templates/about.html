Welcome to the AppRent Manager!

A property finance management app designed for landlords with rental properties. It helps you manage your rental property in a convenient and organized way.

Quick guide to using the app:

  • Step 1 Create an account.
  • Step 2 Add a property to your personal dashboard.
  • Step 3 Create a rental agreement with all the details (e.g., whether bill settlement is included).
  • Step 4 Start managing your property:
    • update payments,
    • add bills,
    • check settlements.

We wish you a pleasant experience using the app!
